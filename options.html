What is the Rewriter API?

Purpose: The Rewriter API takes existing text and transforms it according to your needs - changing tone, removing redundancy, or simplifying language.

How to use: Highlight any text on a webpage, right-click, and choose your preferred rewriting style from the "Tabetha Weaver Rewriting" menu.

üßí Simplify - Explain Like I'm 5

Rephrases text using simpler words and concepts. Excellent for academic papers or complex jargon. Makes content accessible to everyone.

Example: Technical documentation ‚Üí Easy-to-understand explanation

üíº Make Formal - Professional Tone

Increases the formality of text to suit business or academic audiences. Transforms casual writing into professional communication.

Example: Casual message ‚Üí Professional email

üòä Make Casual - Friendly Tone

Lowers formality for social media or internal team communication. Makes formal text more approachable and friendly.

Example: Corporate announcement ‚Üí Social media post

‚úÇÔ∏è De-Redundify - Tighten Up Text

Removes redundancies and less-important information to fit word limits. Makes text more concise while preserving meaning.

Example: 500-word draft ‚Üí 200-word concise version

‚úçÔ∏è Text Writer API

What is the Writer API?

Purpose: The Writer API creates NEW, original content based on your instructions and reference material. It generates fresh text rather than transforming existing content.

How to use: Highlight reference text on a webpage, right-click, choose "Write Draft" from the "Tabetha Weaver Writing" menu, then provide instructions for what you'd like created.

‚úçÔ∏è Write Draft

Generates new content based on selected text and your instructions.

Example Use Cases:

  • Selected product features ‚Üí "Write a marketing description"
  • Selected meeting notes ‚Üí "Draft a thank-you email"
  • Selected article excerpt ‚Üí "Create a blog post introduction"
  • Selected bullet points ‚Üí "Write a professional summary"
